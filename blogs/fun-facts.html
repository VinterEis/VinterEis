Water Can Boil and Freeze at the Same Time

Under certain conditions, such as in a vacuum, water can both boil and freeze simultaneously. This phenomenon, called the "triple point," occurs when the temperature and pressure reach a specific balance, making all three phases of water (liquid, gas, and solid) coexist.

Uranus is the Coldest Planet in Our Solar System

Uranus is the coldest planet in our solar system With minimum temperatures plunging to -224°C (-371°F), Uranus experiences the coldest recorded atmosphere in the solar system. This icy chill is due to its lack of internal heat, making it even colder than the more distant Neptune.

Stars that We See is Actually Their Past Life

When you look up at the night sky, you're witnessing stars as they were in the past. Light from distant stars take thousands or even millions of years to reach us. This means that some stars you see may have already exploded or died, but their light is still traveling through space, giving us a glimpse of their past life.

Tree with the Longest Living is Pinus longaeva

The tree species Pinus longaeva, also known as the Great Basin bristlecone pine, holds the record for the longest-living trees on Earth. Some of these remarkable trees are over 5,000 years old, making them true survivors through millennia of changing climates. Found in the high mountains of the western U.S., they live in harsh environments that actually contribute to their longevity.

With No Food You Can Survive About A Week

While humans can typically survive for about a week without food, the absence of water is far more critical. Dehydration sets in much quicker, and most people can only last around 3 days without water. The human body can survive for an extended period, typically 1 to 2 months, if you have access to water but no food. During this time, your body will start breaking down fat and muscle for energy, but staying hydrated is crucial. Without water, survival becomes impossible in just a few days, but with it, your body can keep functioning even without food for much longer.
